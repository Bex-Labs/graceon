✦ Who We Are

More Than Just Cookies

Graceon was born from a simple belief — that every bite should tell a story. Founded with a passion for exceptional baking and an eye for detail, we set out to create cookies that go beyond the ordinary.

Each recipe is the result of months of careful development, using only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers around the world. From single-origin chocolate to freshly milled flour, we never compromise on quality.

What started as a small home kitchen operation has grown into a beloved artisan brand — but our values have never changed. Every cookie is still handcrafted in small batches, with the same love and attention to detail as the very first one.
