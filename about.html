✦ Who We Are

More Than Just Cookies

At KrispyTast Food, we believe that every cookie should create a moment of joy. For over 10 years, we have been passionately crafting delicious, high-quality cookies and cookie-based desserts that bring people together.

As the proud manufacturer of Graceon Cookies, we are dedicated to creating delightful treats that turn ordinary moments into memorable experiences — from everyday snack cookies to beautifully packaged gifts, event souvenirs, corporate treats, and hospitality offerings.

What began as a passion for baking has grown into a trusted Nigerian food manufacturing business serving individuals, families, organizations, and hospitality brands across the country.
